02

Clusi Control

In development

Home and property service owners lose deals to scattered leads and slow follow-up. Clusi Control is a single command center — pipeline, CRM, estimates and proposals, financials, and prospect research in one place. It flags every lead going cold, forecasts revenue by close probability, simulates win-rate scenarios, and auto-generates insights on which jobs and channels actually make money — while a prospect radar scans your service territories, gathers source evidence, and pushes approved leads straight into the pipeline. A config system adapts the whole thing to each type of business without a rebuild.

About

I came to AI through business, not the other way around.

I started in design at SCAD, then spent a decade hopping around different job types to learn how businesses actually run — at TowneBank I originated mortgages, running pipelines end to end in a systems- and compliance-heavy business; at Tecovas I led a sales floor and store operations; and I founded The Glencove Group to do creative and marketing work for a range of clients.

Somewhere in there, AI stopped being a curiosity and became the thing I build with every day. Now I use it the way I used to use a design brief or a sales pitch: to understand a problem completely and then ship something that solves it. That mix — knowing the business, having the taste, and being able to build — is the whole point.

I'm looking for a team building at the edge of applied AI, where being fluent in both the customer and the product is the job.
